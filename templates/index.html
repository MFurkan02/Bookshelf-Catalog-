Bookshelf Catalog

How to use this tool:

  1. Upload images of your old and new bookshelf layouts.
  2. Click on a detected book spine in the old bookshelf image to search for that book in the new layout.
  3. The system will highlight the matching book in the new bookshelf image.
Important Note: Make sure your uploaded bookshelf images are taken at a clear angle where all book spines are fully visible, ideally perpendicular to the shelf surface.
